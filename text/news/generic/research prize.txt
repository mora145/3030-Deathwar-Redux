BREAKING NEWS: Top Shou De scientist given prize
Shou De
Top Shou De scientist, Brad Chan, wins Council grand prize for innovation in engine design. His revolutionary work on the mathematical re-normalisation of the theoretical quantum multi-coupling doughnut-shaped space-time corpuscles, was revolutionary. 

Speaking at the ceremony he said, 'This award, though it has been given to me, and me alone, truly should go to my team, whose work I modified, whose ideas I tweaked. I am after all, just one man.'
It is now believed this new technological breakthrough may lead to a new wave of high-end propulsion drives. 

The prototype versions of which may be used to probe space beyond the rim quasi-remotely.
