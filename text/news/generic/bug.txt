New Space Bug grips the Galaxy!
Colonial
A new space virus deemed "incurable" by scientists has been steadily spreading throughout jia kun space. 

The 'GU-1-f' virus attacks the respiratory system and completely paralyses before finally killing its victim within hours. In a shocking demonstration of cooperation, the jia kun, shou de and colonial's best medical experts are converging in an attempt to counter this new threat. 

All pilots are advised from picking up any passengers in the sector as the virus is highly contagious. more to come soon.
